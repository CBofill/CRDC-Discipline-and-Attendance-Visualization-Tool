Data are given in four groups:
enrollment
iss (in-school suspensions)
ooss (out-of-school suspensions)
expulsion

Each group is subdivided by race/ethnicity using the naming convention '[group]_[race abbreviation]'.

HI - Hispanic
AM - American Indian/Alaska Native
AS - Asian
HP - Native Hawaiian/Pacific Islander
BL - Black
WH - White
TR - Two or more races

Percentages for each subdivision are given by adding the '_p' suffix.
Composition index for discipline actions are given by adding the '_comp' suffix.
